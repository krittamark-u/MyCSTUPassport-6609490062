CS Firstmeet

CSTU38 Firstmeet is the first event of the Computer Science Department, Faculty of Science and Technology, Thammasat University. The event was held on 6 August 2023 at the Faculty of Science and Technology, Thammasat University, Rangsit Campus. The event was held to welcome new students. And get to know each other in the department. And to get to know the seniors in the department as well.
